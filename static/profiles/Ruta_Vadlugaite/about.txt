Rūta Vadlugaitė (g.1994) baigė Rokiškio meno mokyklą 2012 metais. 2017 metais Vilniaus dailės akademijoje baigė tapybos bakaluro studijas. 2019 metais gavo tapybos magistro diplomą. Dalyvauja parodose nuo 2016 metų.
Atstovaujama Countour Art Gallery, menininkės darbai 2019 metais pristatyti šiuolaikinio meno mugėje ArtMadrid`19,  ONE Project section. 2018-aisiais surengė personalinę parodą „Protarpiai“ bendradarbiaujant su Countour Art Gallery ir Baltic Sotheby‘s International Realty. Gavo pagyrimo sertifikatą už bakalauro baigiamąjį darbą „2016/2017“, už metaforišką tapybos interpretaciją. 2019 metais apdovanota Justino Vienožinskio premija. Jaunojo tapytojo prizo’17 finalininkė ir pirmosios vietos laimėtoja Žabolis Art Project konkurse, tapybos kategorijoje. Kūriniai yra dažnai eksponuojami tarptautinėse meno mugėse: Positions Berlin Art Fair’17 (Selected Positions), ArtVilnius'17, ArtVerona'18, ArtVilnius'18, ArtMadrid'19, ArtVilnius'19.

Rūta Vadlugaitė (1994) finished Rokiškis Art School, in 2012. In 2017 graduated from Vilnius Academy of Arts with Bachelor of Painting. In 2019 received Masters diploma of Painting. Have been participating in exhibitions since 2016.
Represented by Contour Art Gallery Rūta Vadlugaitė‘s works were exhibited at Contemporary Art Fair ArtMadrid`19, ONE Project section, in 2019. In 2018, first solo exhibition "Interlude" was held in collaboration with Contour Art Gallery and Baltic Sotheby`s International Realty. Received the Certificate of Appreciation for the Bachelor thesis "2016/2017" for the metaphorical interpretation of painting. In 2019, received Justinas Vienožinskis award. The  finalist of the Young Painter Prize’17 and the 1st place winner in the Žabolis Art Project competition in the section of painting. Works are often exhibited at international art fairs: Positions Berlin Art Fair’17 (Selected Positions), ArtVilnius'17, ArtVerona'18, ArtVilnius'18, ArtMadrid'19, ArtVilnius'19.
