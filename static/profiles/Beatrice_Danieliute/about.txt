Atvaizdo Slėpinys - Šventųjų atvaizdų kūrimo, poveikio prielaidos. Atvaizdo slėpinio reiškimosi būdus. Spalvos, kompozicijos ir naudojimų simbolių prasmė bei įtaka kultūrinėje ir istorinėje raidoje. Priimamas sprendimas transformuoti ,, Gailestingumo“, ,, Šv. Kazimiero“ paveikslus, sukurti naujus, atliepiančius manąjai prigimčiai ir realiai kasdienybei.

 keliami klausimai bei  ieškomi atsakymai, kur slypi Šventojo vaidmuo istoriniame kontekste, religijoje, kultūroje. Dabartinėje kultūroje atrandami nauji raiškos būdai, nyksta ribos, atvaizdo slėpinys tarpsta naujose psichosocialinių reiškinių sąveikose. The purpose of this work is to discover and analyse the suppositions behind the creation of sacred icon portrayals, as well as the ways how mystery of the icon manifests in the portrayal. It will also explore the meaning and the influence of the colours, compositions, and symbols in the cultural and historical context. I decided to transform the Divine Mercy Image and the Saint Casimir paintings, creating new works, reflecting my own nature and personal life. 
<br>
<br>
The theoretic work part raises questions and looks for answers about the place of the Saint in a historical context, as well as in religion and culture. Today’s culture allows for new ways of expression, which means that the mystery of the sacred portrayal evolves in light of the contemporary psychosocial trends. All of this points to a need for communication between the divine and the human, a dimension of sacrality that is fading. 
<br>
<br>
The purpose of the practical work is to discover the Saint using reconstruction and the transformation of paint layers and details of the painting. By destroying the original image and continuing the creation of the new image, a new form is made, which has unrecognisable elements and responds to the original icon while showing a new interpretation that allows to explore new appreciation of the semblance.
