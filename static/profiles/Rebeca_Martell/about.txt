Rebeca Martell
<br>
<br>
Photography exhibition
<br>
<br>
--
<br>
She has been trained by world respected photographers like Anders Petersen, Antoine Dagata, Alex Webb – to
name a few-in Philadelphia Photo Arts, Jumex Collection, Centro de la Imagen, Museo Rufino Tamayo, Laboratorio
Arte Alameda and Photoespaña.
<br>
Her work has been exhibited at The Barnes Foundation, the Museum of Contemporary Art of Oaxaca, Philadelphia
Photo Arts, in the Gallery of Contemporary Art and Design, XTeresa Arte Actual, Sebastian Foundation, San Pedro
Puebla Art Museum and the National Auditorium, as well as at the Embassy of Mexico in Spain, Belgium, Hungary,
Romania, Holland, Japan and France.
<br>
<br>
Her work has been selected at the 1st and 8th Encuentro Estatal de Arte Contemporáneo in Puebla, Mexico and in
the 6th Biennal de Photojournalism in Mexico.
<br>
<br>
In 2008 she won the 8th Encuentro Estatal de Arte Contemporáneo en Puebla, México, in the advanced young
artists category. Along with the price she got published Recuento, a catalog of her work wich summarizes ten years
of photographic exercise, through various personal projects.
<br>
<br>
Currently engaged in documenting urban cultures as she works as freelance photographer and internacional
correspondent for music and life style magazines such Playboy and Metalhammer.
<br>
<br>
She directs Liliput experimental Gallery.
