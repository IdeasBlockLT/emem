Different takes on memory and will partly consist of 
<br>
 - Global piece: The Ice is Melting at the Pøules: A vigorous organic colour scale that reflects the last 168 years of global temperatures.
<br>
<b>
The title refers to the former Danish Foreign Minister Villy Søvndal's statement “The ice is melting at the pøules”, and that one should consider the alarming consequences of global warming, rather than the minister’s unfortunate pronunciation, which back in 2009 amused the world and overshadowed the message itself. 
<br>
<br>
- An exploration into evolution and genetic and cellular memory.
<br>
<br>
This will be combined with Jonas <a href='/artist/Robotic Folk'>Robotic Folk</a> as an dystopian journey. Cells, DNA and eventually the human race emerge, destroys the climate and perish from the earth - and robots live on, playing folk. music.
