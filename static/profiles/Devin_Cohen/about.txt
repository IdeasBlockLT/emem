Devin Cohen (Philadelphia, U.S., 1985) is a multi-instrumentalist multidisciplinary artist, working with music, visual arts, voice, and poetry. He has recorded several records with poetics atop musicianship. In 2014, under the name Alien Architect, he published the experimental trip hop post hop poetry album Arteria.
<br>
<br>
Devin Cohen has written, exhibited, and performed, across the U.S., Europe, Mexico, Israel, Paris, Iceland, Spain, Germany, Sweden, Romania..
<br>
<br>
His work involves much which is best explored in the work itself, though one theme is: Text as art, not as merely words indicating their definitions, into sound back into text then fragmented into visual visceral abstraction.
