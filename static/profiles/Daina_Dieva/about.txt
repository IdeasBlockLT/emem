Daina Dieva

<br>
Piece: Atminties meditacija / Memory meditation
<br>
<br>
Live sound art / music 
<br>
<br>
Atminties meditacija konstruojama iš tyros, labai tylių garsų, aidų ir signalo fydbeko. Kompozicija atliekama gyvai.
<br>
Memory meditation is based on loops of silence, silent sounds and echoes and signal feedback. Composition is performed live.
<br>
<br>

Daina Dieva is a soundmaker mostly known for creating dense ambient atmospheres and drone soundscapes, however, when possible, she is collaborating with other (sound) artists, amusing herself with creating harsher noises that would physically impact the participants of the piece, as well as, on a year such as this, experimenting with techno-inspired sound structures for contemporary dance.
