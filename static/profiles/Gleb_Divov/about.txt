<br>
Piece: Digitized?
<br>
Sonic/Visual (mixed/multimedia) Art Installation in the basement of Trakų Vokės Manor.
<br>
<br>
Music and soundscapes for this installation composed by the first true computer AI-Composer - Musical Blockchain (
<a href='www.musicalblockchain.com'>musicalblockchain.com</a>).
<br>
<br>
Not everything can be stored in the artificial memory. But humans try to do so.
While world is rushing toward technological revolutions, moods and feelings are memoryless.
<br>
<br>
This installation reminds about the treasure of a moments that cannot be stored in a digital form.
<br>
<br>
Histories won't be tokenized. Yet.
<br>

Gleb (Russia, 1979) is an inventor, visioner, multidisciplinary artist, composer and multi-instrumentalist. Currently lives in Vilnius.

<br>
Gleb is an IT and Music professional with over 20 years of experience, creating pioneering innovations at the intersection of the technologies with music, art, education and other areas.

<br>
He's an Artist, organizer and active member of a global music/art/culture community. Visioner and founder of the Crowded.Place, Digital/Culture center, CryptoArt Hub Baltics/EU and FAYR ecosystem. Inventor of the Musical Blockchain.
